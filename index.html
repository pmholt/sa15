Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Meatballs
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat tomato sauce in a separate pan.
  3. Add cooked meatballs to the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve meatballs and sauce over cooked spaghetti, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Chicken broth
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken in a pot until fully cooked.
  2. Mix flour with chicken broth and milk to form a dough.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough into the simmering chicken broth.
  4. Cook until the dumplings are cooked through.

Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • Apples
  • Pastry dough
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Peel, core, and slice apples.
  2. Line a pie dish with pastry dough.
  3. Fill the pie with sliced apples mixed with sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Cover with another layer of pastry dough and bake until golden brown.
